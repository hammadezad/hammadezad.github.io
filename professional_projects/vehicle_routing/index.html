Vehicle Routing Optimizer

Production routing for a national bottling company with three-phase Gurobi models, warm starts, and flexible quantities.

Daily dispatch planning at cluster and subcluster levels. Warm starts from earlier phases reduce solve time while enforcing realistic limits such as maximum stops, route duration, idle time between stops, and per-location flex windows.

  • Three phases with warm starts and unioned sets for cluster solve
  • Scenario-driven configuration with rotating logs and error files
  • Automated CSV reports for shipments, orders, routes, and scenario stats

Architecture

main.py → run_pipeline()
 ├─ data_loader.load_data_from_dataframes()
 ├─ rate_builder.build_global_rate_dicts()
 ├─ phase0_solver.solve_phase0_subcluster()
 ├─ phase1_solver.solve_phase1_for_cluster()
 ├─ phase2_solver.flex_allocation_and_phase2()
 └─ reports.write_*_csv()

Inputs are scenario-filtered CSVs. Phase-1 unions all subclusters, warms from Phase-0 solutions, and respects capacity, time windows, idle-time, and stop limits derived from rate cards.

var = model.addVar(vtype=GRB.BINARY, name=f"x_{i}_{j}_{k}")

Phase-0 routes seed Phase-1 variables to cut search time and stabilize solutions across daily reruns.

Results and Ops

  • Solve time reduced from about 60 minutes to under 5 minutes for 100+ orders
  • About 15 percent logistics cost savings through better vehicle utilization and flex control
  • Daily reruns with automated report drop for planners and partners
