Vehicle Routing Optimizer

Production pipeline for daily dispatch planning. Three phases with warm starts reduce solve time while enforcing realistic limits like maximum stops, route duration, idle time between stops, and flexible quantities.

  • Gurobi solver with warm starts from earlier phases
  • Scenario-driven configuration and rotating logs
  • Reports for shipments, orders, and routes

Architecture

main.py → run_pipeline()
 ├─ data_loader.load_data_from_dataframes()
 ├─ rate_builder.build_global_rate_dicts()
 ├─ phase0_solver.solve_phase0_subcluster()
 ├─ phase1_solver.solve_phase1_for_cluster()
 ├─ phase2_solver.flex_allocation_and_phase2()
 └─ reports.write_*_csv()
